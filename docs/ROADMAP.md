# WorldView Roadmap

## MVP
- Cesium globe with Google Photorealistic 3D Tiles fallback to terrain.
- Tactical UI shell with left/right panels and HUD overlays.
- Style presets with live selector (Normal + CRT baseline).
- Providers wired for satellites, OpenSky flights, USGS earthquakes.
- Street traffic from Overpass major roads.
- CCTV Mesh with curated public camera list and calibration defaults.

## v1
- Rich satellite details and improved orbit path controls.
- Polished NVG and FLIR shader stages.
- Sequential traffic loading (major -> arterial -> secondary) and adaptive density by FPS.
- Projective CCTV texture onto buildings/terrain.
- Scene planner timeline and smooth cinematic camera transitions.

## v2
- Public plug-in layer ecosystem (weather, transit, bikeshare).
- Offline local session recording and replay.
- Shot-list export for video workflows.
